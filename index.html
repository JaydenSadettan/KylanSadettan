Who I Am

I was born in Seattle, but was raised all around the King county area. Areas such as Burien, Des Moines, Federal Way, and West Seattle. Playing sports my entire life allowed me to create many friendships, strive for goals, and learn the importance of teamwork. My athletic abilities and commitment to education allowed me to advance to the collegiate level and attend Pacific Lutheran University where I played football and was elected as a junior captain. Now as a graduate with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Business Administration, I am ready to take on my next step in life.
